## Production Notes

### Pacing
- Keep transitions smooth and quick
- Use subtle animations (no flashy effects)
- Maintain professional, clean aesthetic
- Fast enough to maintain interest, slow enough to comprehend

### Color Scheme
- Brand colors: Primary blue (#2563EB), Secondary green (#10B981)
- UI should look clean, modern, professional
- Use white space effectively

### Fonts
- Title: Inter Bold, 48px
- Subtitles: Inter Semibold, 24px
- Body: Inter Regular, 16px
- On-screen text: Clear, readable, high contrast

### Voiceover Tone
- Professional but friendly
- Conversational, not salesy
- Clear enunciation
- Moderate pace (not too fast)
- Warm, trustworthy voice
- Gender: Neutral/professional (consider female voice for diversity)

### Screen Recording Settings
- Resolution: 1080p (1920x1080)
- Frame rate: 60fps for smooth UI interactions
- Cursor: Show during interactions, hide during transitions
- Zoom in on important elements
- Use mockup data that looks realistic

### Background Music
- Genre: Corporate/Tech (uplifting, modern)
- Volume: -18dB to -24dB (voiceover should be primary)
- Starts at Scene 1, continues throughout
- Fade out at end

### Sample Data for Recording
Use realistic names and scenarios:
- Contacts: Mix of names suggesting diversity
- Companies: Realistic tech/business companies
- Email subjects: Professional, relevant
- Notes: Authentic-sounding

### Accessibility
- Add closed captions (auto-generate, then edit)
- Include transcript in video description
- Ensure text is high contrast and readable
- Visual elements should support audio (redundancy)

### Distribution Channels
- **Landing page**: Hero section auto-play (muted)
- **YouTube**: Full version with captions
- **LinkedIn**: 30-second cut focusing on B2B value
- **Twitter/X**: 15-second teaser with CTA
- **Product Hunt**: Full 2-minute version
- **Email campaigns**: Embedded or linked
