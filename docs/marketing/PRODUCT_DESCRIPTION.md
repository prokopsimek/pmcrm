## Key Features

### For Landing Pages & Marketing

- **AI-Powered Recommendations**
  Smart suggestions for who to contact, when to reach out, and what to say based on your relationship history and communication patterns.

- **Effortless Contact Import**
  One-click import from Google Contacts, Microsoft 365, and email. Automatic duplicate detection and merging.

- **Intelligent Reminders**
  Set custom reminder frequencies per contact. Never let important relationships fade. Snooze and manage follow-ups with ease.

- **Semantic Search**
  Find contacts using natural language: "investors I met in 2024" or "clients I haven't emailed in months." AI understands context.

- **Email & Calendar Integration**
  Automatically track email interactions and meetings. See complete relationship timelines without manual logging.

- **Privacy-First Design**
  GDPR compliant, EU data residency, optional end-to-end encryption. Your data stays yours. Export or delete anytime.

- **Beautiful Dashboard**
  See your network at a glance: pending follow-ups, AI recommendations, activity timeline, and growth metrics.

- **Team Collaboration**
  Share contacts, assign follow-ups, and collaborate on relationships with your team (Team plan).

- **Cross-Platform**
  Web app optimized for desktop and mobile. Native iOS and Android apps coming Q1 2026.

## Brand Voice & Tone

### Voice Characteristics

- **Professional but approachable**: We're serious about relationships, not stuffy
- **Human-centric**: AI serves humans, not replaces them
- **Transparent**: Clear about capabilities, privacy, and limitations
- **Empowering**: You're in control of your data and relationships

### Writing Guidelines

**DO:**
- Use "you" and "your" (second person)
- Write conversationally
- Be specific with examples
- Show, don't just tell
- Acknowledge pain points
- Use active voice

**DON'T:**
- Oversell or use hyperbole ("revolutionary", "game-changing" sparingly)
- Use jargon without explanation
- Make promises we can't keep
- Sound robotic or corporate
- Ignore user concerns
