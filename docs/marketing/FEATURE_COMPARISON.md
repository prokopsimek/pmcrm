- 500 contact free tier

**Dex Weaknesses:**
- No Android app
- Limited AI (rule-based, not GPT/Claude)
- No semantic search
- More expensive ($20 vs. $12)
- No team features
- US-only (GDPR concerns for EU users)

**Why Choose Personal Network CRM Over Dex:**
1. Superior AI (GPT-4/Claude vs. rules)
2. Semantic search with natural language
3. 40% cheaper ($12 vs. $20)

**When to Choose Dex:**

- Want LinkedIn integration today
- Don't care about advanced AI
