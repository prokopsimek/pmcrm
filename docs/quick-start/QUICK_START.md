# Quick Start Guide

**Get started with Personal Network CRM in 5 minutes**

---

## Step 1: Create Your Account (1 minute)

### Option A: Sign Up with Email

1. Visit [https://app.personalnetworkcrm.com](https://app.personalnetworkcrm.com)
2. Click **"Sign Up"**
3. Enter your details:
   - Email address
   - Password (min. 8 characters)
   - Full name
4. Click **"Create Account"**
5. Check your email and click the verification link

### Option B: Sign Up with Google or Microsoft

1. Visit [https://app.personalnetworkcrm.com](https://app.personalnetworkcrm.com)
2. Click **"Continue with Google"** or **"Continue with Microsoft"**
3. Select your account
4. Authorize the requested permissions
5. You're in!

---

## Step 2: Complete Onboarding (2 minutes)

You'll be guided through a 5-step wizard:

### Wizard Step 1: Profile Setup
- Enter your job title
- Add your company name
- Upload profile picture (optional)
- Click **"Next"**

### Wizard Step 2: Create Workspace
- Name your workspace (e.g., "John's Network")
- Select your industry
- Choose timezone
- Click **"Next"**

### Wizard Step 3: Import Preferences
Choose how you want to add contacts:
- Import from Google Contacts
- Import from Microsoft 365
- Add manually
- Click **"Next"** to skip for now

### Wizard Step 4: Reminder Settings
- Set default reminder frequency: **Monthly** (recommended for beginners)
- Enable email notifications
- Click **"Next"**

### Wizard Step 5: AI Preferences
- Enable AI recommendations: **Yes** (recommended)
- Set recommendation frequency: **Weekly**
- Click **"Complete Setup"**

Congratulations! You're now on your dashboard.

---

## Step 3: Import Your First Contacts (1 minute)

### Quick Import from Google Contacts

1. From your dashboard, click **"Import Contacts"**
2. Select **"Google Contacts"**
3. Click **"Connect Google Contacts"**
4. Sign in to your Google account
5. Click **"Allow"** to grant permissions
6. Select which contacts to import:
   - **All contacts** (recommended for first import)
   - Or specific groups
7. Configure duplicate handling: **"Update existing contacts"**
8. (Optional) Add a tag: **"Google Import"**
9. Click **"Start Import"**

Wait for the import to complete (you'll see a progress bar).

### Quick Import from Microsoft 365

1. From your dashboard, click **"Import Contacts"**
2. Select **"Microsoft 365"**
3. Click **"Connect Microsoft 365"**
4. Sign in to your Microsoft account
5. Click **"Accept"** to grant permissions
6. Select contacts to import: **"All contacts"**
7. Configure duplicate handling: **"Skip duplicates"**
8. (Optional) Add a tag: **"Microsoft Import"**
9. Click **"Start Import"**

### Manual Contact Entry

Not ready to import? Add a few contacts manually:

1. Click **"Add Contact"** button (top right)
2. Fill in basic info:
   - First name
   - Last name
   - Email
   - Company
   - Job title
3. Add a tag (e.g., "Client", "Friend", "Investor")
4. Click **"Save Contact"**

## Step 4: Explore Your Dashboard (1 minute)

Your dashboard shows:

### Contacts Overview
- **Total contacts**: See your imported contacts
- **Added this week**: Track growth
- **Active integrations**: Connected services

### Follow-Up Queue
- **Today's follow-ups**: Contacts to reach out to today
- **This week**: Upcoming follow-ups
- **Overdue**: Missed follow-ups (if any)

### AI Recommendations
- Smart suggestions for who to contact
- Best times to reach out
- Relationship insights

### Quick Actions
- **Add Contact**: Create new contact
- **Import More**: Import from other sources
- **Search**: Find contacts quickly

---

## Step 5: Set Up Your First Reminder (30 seconds)

Let's ensure you stay in touch with an important contact:

1. Click on any contact from your contacts list
2. Click **"Edit"** button
3. Scroll to **"Reminder Settings"**
4. Choose reminder frequency:
   - **Weekly**: For close contacts
   - **Monthly**: For regular contacts
   - **Quarterly**: For occasional contacts
5. Click **"Save Contact"**

You'll now receive reminders to follow up with this contact!

## Next Steps

Now that you're set up, here's what to explore next:

### Connect More Integrations
- **Email Sync**: Track email interactions automatically
  - Navigate to Settings > Integrations > Email
  - Connect Gmail or Outlook
  - Enable interaction tracking

- **Calendar Sync**: Log meetings automatically
  - Navigate to Settings > Integrations > Calendar
  - Connect Google Calendar or Microsoft Calendar
  - Enable meeting tracking

### Organize Your Contacts
- **Create Tags**: Settings > Tags > Create New Tag
  - Examples: "VIP", "Client", "Investor", "Friend"
- **Use Filters**: Filter contacts by tags, company, location
- **Create Smart Lists**: Save frequently used filters

### Try AI Features
- **Semantic Search**: Use natural language to find contacts
  - Example: "investors I met in 2024"
  - Example: "clients I haven't emailed in a month"

- **AI Recommendations**: Review daily AI suggestions
  - Check Dashboard > AI Recommendations
  - Give thumbs up/down to improve recommendations

### Customize Your Experience
- **Dashboard Widgets**: Customize > Drag and drop widgets
- **Notification Settings**: Settings > Notifications
- **Theme**: Settings > Appearance (Dark/Light mode)

### Set Up Team Collaboration (Team Plan)
- **Invite Team Members**: Settings > Team > Invite
- **Share Contacts**: Enable team access to contacts
- **Assign Follow-ups**: Delegate tasks to team members

## Tips for Success

1. **Import All Your Contacts Early**: The more contacts you have, the better AI recommendations work

2. **Set Realistic Reminder Frequencies**: Don't overwhelm yourself
   - Start with monthly reminders
   - Adjust based on relationship importance

3. **Use Tags Consistently**: Create a tag system and stick to it
   - Relationship type (Client, Partner, Friend)
   - Status (Active, Inactive, Prospect)
   - Source (Conference, LinkedIn, Referral)

4. **Review Dashboard Daily**: Spend 2 minutes each morning reviewing:
   - Today's follow-ups
   - AI recommendations
   - Recent activity

5. **Provide AI Feedback**: Thumbs up/down on recommendations helps personalize the AI to your style

6. **Enable All Integrations**: More data = Better insights
   - Google/Microsoft contacts
   - Email sync
   - Calendar sync

7. **Export Your Data Regularly**: Settings > Privacy > Export Data
   - Keep a backup
   - Ensures GDPR compliance

## Common First-Time Questions

**Q: How many contacts should I import?**
A: Import all your contacts initially. You can always add tags and filters to focus on specific groups.

**Q: Will importing create duplicates?**
A: Our duplicate detection is very accurate. Choose "Skip duplicates" or "Update existing" during import.

**Q: How often should I set reminders?**
A: Start with:
- Weekly: Top 10 most important contacts
- Monthly: Regular contacts (50-100 people)
- Quarterly: Everyone else

**Q: Do I have to act on every AI recommendation?**
A: No! Dismiss recommendations that don't make sense. The AI learns from your feedback.

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Yes. We're GDPR compliant with end-to-end encryption. We never sell your data.

**Q: Can I use this on mobile?**
A: The web app works on mobile browsers. Native iOS/Android apps coming soon!

**Q: How do I get help?**
A: Click the chat icon (bottom right) or email support@personalnetworkcrm.com
